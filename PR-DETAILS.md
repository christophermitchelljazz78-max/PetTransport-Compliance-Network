# Smart Contract Implementation for Pet Transport Compliance Network\n\n## Overview\n\nThis pull request introduces the foundational smart contracts for the PetTransport-Compliance-Network, a comprehensive blockchain-based system designed to ensure safe, humane pet transportation through transparency, accountability, and immutable record-keeping.\n\n## Features Added\n\n### 🏢 Carrier Licensing Registry Contract\n- **Purpose**: Central registry for pet transport carrier verification and management\n- **Key Capabilities**:\n  - Carrier registration with licensing verification\n  - Insurance policy tracking and validation \n  - Safety inspection status monitoring\n  - Compliance scoring and rating system\n  - Administrative controls for suspension/reactivation\n\n### 📡 Route and Telemetry Proof Contract\n- **Purpose**: Real-time monitoring and verification of transport conditions\n- **Key Capabilities**:\n  - Environmental condition tracking (temperature, humidity)\n  - GPS location verification with timestamps\n  - Movement and acceleration monitoring\n  - Automated violation detection and alerting\n  - Cryptographically signed telemetry data\n\n## Technical Implementation\n\n### Smart Contract Architecture\n- **Platform**: Stacks blockchain using Clarity smart contracts\n- **Design Pattern**: Modular architecture with separation of concerns\n- **Security Features**: \n  - Owner-based access control\n  - Input validation and error handling\n  - Immutable audit trails\n  - Cryptographic data integrity\n\n### Contract Functions\n\n#### Carrier Licensing Registry\n- `register-carrier`: Register new transport providers\n- `update-insurance`: Update insurance information\n- `record-inspection`: Record safety inspection results\n- `suspend-carrier`: Administrative suspension functionality\n- `is-carrier-authorized`: Real-time authorization checking\n\n#### Route and Telemetry Proof  \n- `start-route`: Initialize new transport routes\n- `submit-telemetry`: Record environmental and location data\n- `complete-route`: Finalize transport completion\n- `create-alert`: Generate violation notifications\n- `get-route-safety-score`: Calculate transport safety metrics\n\n## Data Structures\n\n### Carrier Registry\n```clarity\n- carriers: Core carrier information and status\n- carrier-ratings: Safety and performance metrics\n- authorized-inspectors: Certified inspection authorities\n```\n\n### Telemetry System\n```clarity\n- routes: Transport route definitions and status\n- telemetry-readings: Environmental and location data\n- route-alerts: Violation and incident notifications\n- route-summaries: Aggregated transport analytics\n```\n\n## Safety and Compliance Features\n\n### Environmental Monitoring\n- **Temperature Range**: 15.0°C - 25.0°C (safe range for most pets)\n- **Humidity Range**: 30% - 70% relative humidity\n- **Real-time Violations**: Automatic detection and alerting\n- **Historical Analytics**: Trend analysis and reporting\n\n### Accountability Measures\n- **Immutable Records**: All data permanently stored on blockchain\n- **Digital Signatures**: Cryptographic verification of all readings\n-
